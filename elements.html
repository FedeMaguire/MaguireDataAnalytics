CNC STUDIO

3D PRODUCT DESIGN / CNC / CAD-CAM

history

CNCstudio, founded in 2010, is a precision-focused CNC solutions business tailored for the specific cutting needs of the naval and furniture industries. As the founder and lead designer of CNCstudio, my skills grew across 3D design tools, Gcode programming, Mach3, Rhino, CAD/CAM, Arduino, analytical skills, problem-solving, and teamwork. Our goal is to bring a blend of creativity and precision to every project, ensuring high quality results for our clients in these specialized industries.
